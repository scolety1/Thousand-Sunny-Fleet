# Future Plans

These are promising ship and product ideas to revisit after rate limits reset and the current fleet is ready for another planned run.

## Restaurant Menu Engineer

Perfect fit for the restaurant demo ecosystem.

Core idea:
- Menu margin modeling
- Prep load scoring
- Ingredient overlap analysis
- Popularity matrix
- Item engineering recommendations

Why it matters:
- Turns restaurant demos from pretty websites into operational decision tools.
- Pairs naturally with RestaurantProfitLab, OrderPilot, ShiftPlate, and Cellar demos.

## Shift Staffing Optimizer

Perfect fit for the restaurant demo ecosystem.

Core idea:
- Demand forecast
- Labor cost model
- Coverage gap detection
- Callout scenarios
- Role-by-role staffing recommendations

Why it matters:
- Makes manager-facing restaurant tools feel genuinely useful.
- Could connect with ShiftLedger, ShiftPlate, and RestaurantProfitLab.

## School Semester Planner

Potential EasyLife feature or companion ship.

Core idea:
- Upload/import a school schedule or syllabus
- Extract expected deadlines
- Add calendar events
- Add assignment/task reminders
- Track grade weights and exam windows
- Estimate study load and burnout risk

Why it matters:
- Reduces manual school setup inside EasyLife.
- Fits the "high tech but simple" EasyLife direction: upload once, then let the app structure the semester.

## Personal Energy Dashboard

Potential EasyLife feature.

Core idea:
- Combine sleep, workouts, tasks, calendar, and open time
- Forecast daily capacity
- Show overload warnings
- Suggest lighter/heavier task planning based on energy

Why it matters:
- Makes EasyLife feel adaptive instead of just organized.
- Connects EasyWorkout, EasyCalendar, EasyList, and EasyHQ into one useful planning layer.

## Trip Value Lab

Potential standalone analysis ship.

Core idea:
- Flight, hotel, food, activity, and transportation budget
- Time cost and itinerary tradeoffs
- Value comparisons across trip options
- Scenario planning for budget vs experience

Why it matters:
- Strong formula/data-heavy tool idea.
- Could become a useful personal decision lab alongside FinanceDecisionLab and LifeCapacity.
