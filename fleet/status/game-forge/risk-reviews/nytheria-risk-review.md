# Game Risk Review - Nytheria

Generated by TSF Game Forge V1. Evidence only; not executable authority or approval.

## Top Risks

- Trying to build full living world too early.
- AI rewriting lore without canon approval.
- Building story before canon tiers.
- Building a full engine before toy simulations.
- Unbounded NPC memory.
- No save/load model.
- No world-state schema.
- Confusing old Nytheris vs new Nytheria source truth.
