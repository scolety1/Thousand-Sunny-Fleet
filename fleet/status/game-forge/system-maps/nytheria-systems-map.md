## Required Flow Coverage

- Player actions update the Player Action Ledger.
- Faction goals alter region control and timeline pressure.
- Gods/faith influence rumors, regions, and consequences.
- Rumors mutate from events and NPC memory.
- Timeline events and world state snapshots must be testable.
