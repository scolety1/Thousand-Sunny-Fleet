            Reviewed previews can become bounded governed TSF-local read-only missions through canonical controls. Canonical TIM_REQUIRED requests allow exact approval, denial, or bounded clarification responses only. A response may create a new governed revision; the prior worker is never resumed. Arbitrary repositories and general commands; plugins, credentials, deployment, push, merge, production access, and expanded authority remain unavailable. Submission is not approval; worker completion is not admission; the canonical approval ledger, mission revision, and admission receipt remain authoritative.
